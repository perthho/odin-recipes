Sev Tomato

Description

Sev Tomato is a popular Gujarati dish made with tomatoes cooked into a tangy, slightly sweet curry and topped with crunchy sev (crispy chickpea flour noodles).

It is usually served with roti, paratha, or rice.

Ingredients

  • 3 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup sev
  • Fresh coriander leaves

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard and cumin seeds.
  2. Add onions and cook until golden brown.
  3. Add green chilies and ginger-garlic paste.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Mix in turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, salt, and sugar.
  6. Cook until the curry thickens slightly.
  7. Add sev just before serving.
  8. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
