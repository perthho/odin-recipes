Aloo Paratha

Description

Aloo Paratha is a famous North Indian flatbread stuffed with a spicy mashed potato filling. It is commonly eaten for breakfast with butter, curd, or pickle.

Ingredients

For Dough

  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • Water as needed
  • 1 teaspoon oil
  • Salt

For Filling

  • 3 boiled potatoes
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Mix wheat flour, salt, oil, and water to make soft dough.
  2. Mash the boiled potatoes in a bowl.
  3. Add green chili, cumin powder, garam masala, coriander, and salt.
  4. Roll a small dough ball and place potato filling inside.
  5. Seal and roll gently into a flat circle.
  6. Cook on a hot pan with oil or butter until both sides are golden brown.
  7. Serve hot with curd or pickle.
