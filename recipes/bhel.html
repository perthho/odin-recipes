Bhel

Description

Bhel, also known as Bhel Puri, is a crunchy Indian street food snack made with puffed rice, vegetables, chutneys, and sev. It is spicy, sweet, and tangy.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cups puffed rice
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons tamarind chutney
  • 1 boiled potato (chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon green chutney
  • 1/2 cup sev
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Chaat masala
  • Lemon juice

Steps

  1. Add puffed rice to a large mixing bowl.
  2. Mix in onion, tomato, and boiled potato.
  3. Add tamarind chutney and green chutney.
  4. Sprinkle chaat masala and lemon juice.
  5. Mix everything quickly.
  6. Top with sev and coriander leaves.
  7. Serve immediately to keep it crispy.
